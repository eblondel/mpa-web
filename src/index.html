Welcome to the Protected Area Impact Map VRE Data Explorer

This application allows users to query a rich database to report on the presence of natural features and human usages of managed areas (i.e.: Marine Protected Areas) relative to a target region of interest (i.e.: Exclusive Economic Zone). The target region to be reported on can be defined in the menu below or by selecting a region on the map. A custom area can also be uploaded to this service and reported on. The different datasets included in the report are listed on the right. The interactive map can be used to get an overview of the spatial distribution of different features.
